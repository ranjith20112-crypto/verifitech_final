FAQs — Employment Verification

For defunct companies, we use alternative verification methods including EPFO/UAN records (which maintain employment history regardless of company status), references from former colleagues or managers, relieving letters and pay slips authentication, and physical address verification of the former company location. We also check merger/acquisition records to trace the successor entity responsible for employment records.

Our system employs multiple fraud detection layers: (1) Company database cross-check to verify if the organization is registered and operational, (2) Email domain validation to ensure HR emails are from official company domains, (3) Telephonic verification using our database of verified HR contacts rather than candidate-provided numbers, (4) EPFO record matching to confirm UAN contributions, and (5) Physical address verification for suspicious small companies. Discrepancies are flagged with red alerts in the final report.

Employment gaps are flagged for additional scrutiny but are not automatically considered negative. We collect documentation supporting the gap reason (further education, health reasons, family care, entrepreneurship, etc.) and verify these claims where possible. For BFSI and security-sensitive roles, gaps exceeding 6 months require detailed explanation and supporting documents. Our report categorizes gaps as "explained" or "unexplained" to help you make informed decisions.

International verifications typically take 7-10 business days depending on the country and company response time. We have dedicated teams for US/UK (3-5 days), Middle East (5-7 days), and APAC regions (7-10 days). For urgent requirements, we offer expedited verification through premium channels. We also maintain partnerships with international verification agencies to accelerate responses from major global corporations.

All employment data is encrypted using AES-256 standard both in transit and at rest. Access is restricted to authorized verification executives with role-based permissions. We obtain explicit candidate consent before contacting current employers to protect their privacy. HR contacts receive secure, time-limited verification links rather than sensitive documents via email. Our systems are ISO 27001 certified and GDPR compliant, with regular third-party security audits.

Yes, we verify CTC components including fixed salary, variable pay, and benefits as part of our standard verification. However, some companies have policies restricting detailed salary disclosure and will only confirm employment dates and designation. In such cases, we request Form 16, salary slips, or bank statements from the candidate as supporting evidence. Salary verification helps identify candidates who may have misrepresented their previous compensation during negotiations.
